About Me

I am a detail-oriented professional upskilling for new opportunities as a Fullstack developer. I have recently completed a 20 week immersive course in Java. I possess expertise in OOP design principles, Client-Server Programming (PostgreSQL, Spring, JPA, REST), Cloud and Microservices deployments, and DevOps (eg. CI/CD).

I bring graduate-level education and training in linguistics and formal logic, with some degree of training in NLP methodologies in Python and statistical processing with R.

In my previous role, I managed an industry standard database of gambling industry information for 6 years at Information Technology Systems, Inc. Additionally, I created online content for the company, and led several successful company initiatives, including adding and tracking available data on top executives at companies in our database using a variety of publicly accessible data sources.

In my role at Amazon Data Services, I have brought several workflows from customer concept to processes executable by associates through collaborative creation and refinement of documentation, conventions, and guidelines for human annotation. I have additionally led quality audits for multiple mission critical LLM workflows, designed and implemented quality intervention mechanisms to remediate faults in human-in-the-loop processes, and worked closely with stakeholders across teams to align on quality goals.

I firmly believe my strong academic and tech industry background, coupled with my passion for learning new skills and building scalable, modular solutions, will be valuable assets in any transition to a development role. I am eager to learn while taking on new challenges and projects, and to take the opportunity to collaborate with senior developers to hone my technical skills and build great things together with my future team!
